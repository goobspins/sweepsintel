MyPrize has a trap in it that doesnt get explained anywhere, and Ive watched people fall into it repeatedly.

When you open the game library, the MyPrize-branded slots are right there at the top. The platforms own content, front and center. It feels like a natural place to play, especially if youre just getting started and havent gone looking for specific wash games yet. And youd think that playing the casinos own branded games would count toward your playthrough.

It doesnt. Not even a little.

MyPrize original games are excluded entirely from bonus wagering. You can spin them for hours, clear hundreds of rounds, and your playthrough requirement will not move. The platform isnt hiding this, its in the terms, but the terms are buried and assume you already know what youre looking for. Someone opening MyPrize for the first time and seeing their branded games headlining the library has no reason to suspect those games are functionally worthless for clearing.

The actual wash game is Pigsby. Ten cents a spin, roughly 93% observed RTP, low variance, does what you need it to do. If you want a second option, look through the eligible games list in the terms and find something in that same range. But Pigsby is where most people land.

This matters more on MyPrize than it would on some other platform because MyPrize is the place a lot of players want money to end up. Instant redemptions under a certain amount, good streak system, VIP rewards that actually accumulate, celebrity partnerships that make the platform feel stable. Its a destination in most serious rotations. But if youre bleeding SC on ineligible games trying to get to redemption, youre eating losses you didnt have to take.

Before you buy anything on a new casino, pull up the terms and find the bonus wagering section. Look for the eligible games list, and look specifically for exclusions. MyPrize lists them. Most casinos do if you dig for it.

The games at the top of the library arent always the ones that count.
