# No, Sweepstakes Casinos Dont All Take Weeks to Pay Out

I see this complaint in every Discord server and every Reddit thread. "I requested a redemption five days ago and Im still waiting, these sites are a scam." And I get it, waiting for money youve already earned feels terrible. But the idea that all sweepstakes casinos are slow payers is just wrong, and believing it will cost you real time and real money because youll avoid platforms that would have paid you in minutes.

The actual range is instant on one end and 7+ business days on the other. Thats not a small gap. Thats the difference between buying groceries tonight and forgetting you even requested the redemption by the time it finally hits your bank.

## The fast ones

[Global Poker](/casinos/global-poker) is the one I point every new player to when they ask about speed. Gift card redemptions are instant. You click redeem, you get a Visa gift card code, you spend it. Ive redeemed at 11 PM on a Sunday and had the code in under a minute. No bank processing window, no compliance team eyeballing your account, no waiting period at all.

[MyPrize](/casinos/myprize) is the other one worth mentioning for speed. Their redemptions have consistently come through same-day for me, Ive tracked maybe 15 of them now and the longest was about six hours. Most were under two. Their batched playthrough system is a little unusual because each SC credit has its own counter, which threw me off at first, but the actual cashout speed is hard to beat.

## The slow ones

This is where the myth comes from. Some casinos genuinely do take 5-7 business days for ACH, and if your first experience happens to be with one of those I understand why youd assume theyre all like that.

[Chumba Casino](/casinos/chumba-casino) is the biggest name in the space and their redemption times are fine. Not great. My last three Chumba redemptions averaged about 4.2 business days, which is honestly pretty normal for ACH processing, your regular bank transfer from Venmo takes about the same time. But if you just came from Global Poker where you got paid in 60 seconds, four days feels like forever.

[Fortune Coins](/casinos/fortune-coins) has been middle of the road in my experience. Two to four business days, though Ive only done a handful of redemptions there so my sample size isnt huge. Ive seen some people in the community say theyve gotten faster recently but I cant confirm that personally.

## Why it matters for your rotation

Redemption speed should factor into which casinos you prioritize in your daily rotation. If youre farming SC across a bunch of platforms (and you should be, thats the whole [getting started](/getting-started) playbook), the ones that pay faster deserve more of your attention. Its not just about convenience either. Faster redemption means faster reinvestment if youre running multiple accounts across families, and it means less money sitting in limbo where you cant touch it.

Ive started treating redemption speed as a first-class metric when I evaluate casinos, right up there with daily bonus value and promoban risk. A casino can have an amazing daily bonus but if it takes eight days to get your money out that changes the math on whether its worth logging in every morning.
